Speaker Cheat Sheet

1. Access the Speaker Control Page

  1. Open your browser and go to:
    http://your-server:3000/speaker.html

2. Enter the Speaker Token

  1. When prompted, enter the Speaker Token provided by the system administrator or found in the backend console or .env file.
  2. If you enter the wrong token, you will be prompted again.

3. Control the Presentation

  • Next/Previous: Advance or go back slides using the Next and Previous buttons.
  • Open Poll: On a poll slide, click Open Poll to allow audience voting.
  • Close Poll: Click Close Poll to stop voting. You can re-open the poll if needed.
  • Poll Results: View live poll results as a bar chart below the controls.
  • Slide Number: See your current position (e.g., "Slide 3 of 10").

4. Tips for a Smooth Presentation

  • Test your slides and polls before the session.
  • Keep your Speaker Token private‚Äîanyone with it can control the show.
  • Remind the audience to scan the QR code or visit the short voting URL for polls.
  • If you lose connection, refresh the page and re-enter your token.
  • To reset the token, ask your admin to update the .env file and restart the backend.
Need help? Contact your system administrator or refer to the project README for troubleshooting.
